# ProcessX - Project Overview

## Project Name
**ProcessX** - Bookkeeping CRM System

## Vision
Transform the existing tax calculator into a comprehensive bookkeeping and CRM platform for South African businesses, enabling them to manage finances, track expenses, manage teams, and stay tax-compliant.

## Current State
- Single-page tax calculator (Next.js 16 + React 19)
- Two calculators: Salaried Employee & Business Owner
- South African 2025/26 tax year calculations
- No authentication, database, or user accounts

## Target State
- Multi-tenant SaaS platform
- User authentication and company accounts
- Team collaboration with role-based access
- Expense management system
- Income tracking and invoicing
- Financial reporting and analytics
- Tax compliance tools
- Integration with existing tax calculators

## Core Value Propositions
1. **Simplified Bookkeeping**: Easy-to-use expense and income tracking
2. **Team Collaboration**: Multi-user access with permission controls
3. **Tax Compliance**: Built-in South African tax tools and calculations
4. **Financial Insights**: Real-time reports and analytics
5. **Growth Ready**: Scalable from sole proprietor to growing business

## Target Market
- South African small to medium businesses
- Sole proprietors and freelancers
- Small Business Corporations (SBCs)
- Startups and growing companies
- Businesses needing simple bookkeeping without complex accounting software

## Success Metrics
- User registrations and active companies
- Monthly active users (MAU)
- Average expenses tracked per company
- Report generation frequency
- Team collaboration adoption rate
- Customer retention and satisfaction
